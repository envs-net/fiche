envs.net | TermBin


Netcat-based command line pastebin.



USAGE

You can POST a Text:
    echo just testing! | nc tb.envs.net 9999
Content of a File:
    cat ~/some_file.txt | nc tb.envs.net 9999
Or your Terminal Output:
    ls -la | nc termbin.com 9999


ACCEPTABLE USE POLICY

Please do not post any informations that 
may violate law (login/password lists, email lists, personal information).
Life span of single paste is one month. Older pastes are deleted.


REQUIREMENTS

There is only one thing you need to use this service - netcat.
Netcat is available on most platforms, including Windows, Mac OS X and Linux.

If you run a server and like this site, clone it! Centralization is bad.
Source here: https://github.com/envs-net/fiche.
You can also support it financially via Liberapay.



ALIAS

To make your life easier, you can add alias to your .bashrc on Linux
and .bash_profile on Mac OS X. Just remember to reset your terminal session after that.
echo 'alias tb="nc tb.envs.net 9999"' >> .bashrc
or
echo 'alias tb="nc tb.envs.net 9999"' >> .bash_profile

now you can use:
echo less typing now! | tb


ABUSE

If you would like to request permanent deletion, please
send an email to hostmaster@envs.net.

Please allow up to 24 hours for a response.
